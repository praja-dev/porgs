## PORGS — Product Specification

## Overview

**PORGS (Praja Organizations)** is an open-source **Modular Civic Framework** designed to manage the people, work, and
transparency of community and government organizations. From a neighbourhood youth society, a temple, or a school, to a
village officer division and its administrative hierarchy up to the office of the head of national government, PORGS
provides the digital scaffolding necessary for collective action.

The core philosophy of PORGS is **Emergent Governance**. Instead of a rigid, top-down implementation, PORGS allows
organizations to digitize at their own pace. A single village official can deploy an instance today; tomorrow, that
instance can "plug in" to a neighboring village or a parent government division, forming a resilient, collaborative
network.

- **Organizational Sovereignty:** Each organization maintains absolute ownership of its data, members, and internal
  discussions.
- **Transparency by Design:** PORGS enables organizations to "open the blinds" by exposing specific tasks and
  responsibilities to the public, fostering radical accountability and trust.
- **Fluid Membership:** The system tracks the real-world flow of community life, allowing identities (people or AI) to
  move seamlessly between different organizations and roles over time.
- **Action-Oriented Knowledge:** By anchoring threaded discussions and wiki-style notes directly to tasks and missions,
  PORGS ensures every decision is backed by clear, searchable context.
